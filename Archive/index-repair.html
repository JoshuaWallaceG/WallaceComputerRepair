About Me

Hi, my name’s Josh! I started Wallace Computer Repair to bring clear, honest, in-home tech help to people in the Tri-Cities.

I’ve loved working with technology from a young age. I built my first computer at 11 and later developed an interest in restoring historic computers. That curiosity turned into years of hands-on experience troubleshooting, repairing, and maintaining all kinds of devices.

Alongside running Wallace Computer Repair, I’ve worked in IT support roles at two universities. Those experiences taught me that tech support works best when it’s patient, practical, and stress-free.

That’s the same approach I bring to every in-home visit today. If your tech is giving you trouble, I’d be glad to help get it running again.
